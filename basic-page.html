Intro to Pixel Art

Pixel art is a form of digital art where images are created and edited at the pixel level. It is commonly used in retro-style games and is known for its crisp, grid-like aesthetic. Mastering pixel art requires understanding shading, color palettes, and working within resolution constraints.

This guide will walk you through the fundamentals of sprite creation, tilesets, and animation techniques used in classic and modern indie games.
